Privacy Policy

Clearate respects your privacy and operates on a principle of minimal data collection. We collect Anonymous usage data through Firebase to improve app performance and stability.

  • No personal identifiable information (PII) is ever collected or stored.
  • Currency rate data is cached locally on your device for offline access.
  • We never sell or share data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Your trust is our primary asset. We maintain rigorous security standards to ensure the integrity of the data provided to you.
