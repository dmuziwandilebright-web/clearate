About

Clearate

Financial Truth for Every Zimbabwean

Clearate was built in Plumtree, Zimbabwe by an 18-year-old developer who got tired of standing in a shop not knowing whether the exchange rate being quoted was honest. Not just for buyers — for anyone who touches more than one currency. The vendor pricing their tomatoes. The person changing rands at the bureau. The tuckshop owner working out what to charge. The family receiving money from abroad. If a rate is involved and you want to know if it is fair, Clearate tells you in three seconds.

Every feature exists because a real Zimbabwean needed it. The rates come directly from the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe. The Price Check works offline. The QR sharing works without internet. Nothing in this app is decoration — it is all here because it solves something real.
