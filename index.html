About Clearate

Born in Plumtree to make money clearer for everyone

Clearate was built in Plumtree, Zimbabwe by an 18-year-old founder who saw families, vendors, and students wrestling with fast-changing rates and unclear prices. The idea was simple: put transparent financial checks into a free app that works for real life in Zimbabwe.

  • 🚀Designed for quick checks at shops, markets, schools, and border towns.
  • 🔒Kept simple so anyone can use it without signing up or paying.
  • 📱Built for Android-first access where many Zimbabweans already work and learn.
